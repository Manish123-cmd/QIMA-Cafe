The Beginning

Qima Cafe was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the way people experience coffee and pastries.

Our founder, inspired by traditional European baking techniques and modern innovation, set out to create a space where craftsmanship meets creativity. Every recipe has been perfected through countless hours of dedication and refinement.

From our roots in Yemen — the birthplace of coffee — through Colombia and Ecuador, Qima Cafe was founded with a bold purpose: to transform every cup into a story of craftsmanship, sustainability and exceptional flavour.

We partner directly with smallholder farmers across more than 7,000 producers, ensuring full traceability from tree to cup. Each coffee tells a story — one of care, respect, and creativity.

At Qima Cafe’s London location, you’ll not only taste exceptional coffees — you’ll feel it in the design and atmosphere. Warm tones, textured walls, and natural hues reflect our story of authenticity and innovation.

Our Mission

At Qima, we believe that exceptional pastries have the power to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. Our mission is to craft each item with the same care and attention as if it were being made for our own family.

Every pastry, loaf, and cup of coffee tells a story — of time-honored recipes refined through patience, skill, and passion. We take pride in blending artisanal techniques with a modern approach to flavor, ensuring every bite is both familiar and exciting.

We’re deeply committed to sustainability — sourcing our ingredients locally and maintaining ethical partnerships with our suppliers. Our kitchen is a place where innovation and tradition meet harmoniously.

Because for us, baking isn’t just a process — it’s a celebration of community, culture, and creativity.
